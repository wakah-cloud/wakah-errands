About Us

Who is Wakah Errands?

Welcome to Wakah Errands,
your ultimate partner for convenience and efficiency in Kenya. Since our founding in 2019, we've been dedicated to transforming the way individuals and businesses manage their day-to-day tasks. Our mission is simple: to save you time, so you can focus on what matters most to you. Every errand is personal to us because we understand that each task has its unique importance. Professional Team: Our staff is trained, vetted, and committed to excellence. Transparency: We keep you updated every step of the way, ensuring peace of mind.

Community Engagement:

We're not just a service; we're part of the community, actively engaging through social media to understand and meet your evolving needs.
